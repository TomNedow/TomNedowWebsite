Exercise Library

Welcome to my exercise database! This was my very first project when I started diving into coding, and it’s my pride and joy. Think of it as your personal exercise encyclopedia with a twist—complete with detailed descriptions, images, and tips to help you crush your fitness goals. Whether you’re trying to master a squat or mix up your routine, I’ve got your back with this awesome resource. I built it with the goal of making fitness accessible and fun for everyone—no matter if you're a newbie or a seasoned pro. Let’s just say, it’s a lot more than just a list of exercises, it’s a workout buddy in digital form.
